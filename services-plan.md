# Services Pages Implementation Plan (AFOB-3)

## Pages to Create:

### 1. Main Services Page (`services.html`)
- Overview of all service categories
- Quick links to individual service pages
- Pricing ranges for each category
- CTA buttons to individual pages

### 2. Individual Service Pages:
- `services/wigs.html` - Wig Styling services
- `services/braiding.html` - Braiding services
- `services/cuts.html` - Hair Cuts services
- `services/nails.html` - Nail Services

## Each Service Page Must Include:
- Hero section with service category name
- Detailed service descriptions
- Individual service items with:
  - Service name
  - Full description
  - Pricing (range or fixed)
  - Duration estimate
  - Before/after photos (from assets/images)
- Gallery section with relevant images
- CTA buttons to book appointments
- Schema markup for SEO

## Services Details:

### Wig Styling ($150-400, 2-4 hours)
